Project Definition & Objectives

Project Definition

Technospy aims to develop a comprehensive attendance platform that enables employees to mark attendance through mobile apps, biometric devices, and geo-location, while offering administrators real-time tracking, reporting, and management tools to streamline operations.

Primary Objectives

  • Enable employees to mark attendance via GPS, facial recognition, QR codes, or fingerprint devices with accuracy and convenience
  • Provide real-time access to attendance logs and reports for administrators and employees alike
  • Automate leave management, scheduling, and alerts to enhance productivity
  • Securely store sensitive attendance data with compliance to privacy standards
  • Create a scalable platform that can support future enhancements like payroll integration, multi-location support, and AI-driven insights

Example Use Cases

  • A field technician marking attendance through GPS from remote sites
  • An HR manager tracking employee attendance across multiple departments and locations
  • Employees accessing their attendance history and submitting leave requests through the mobile app

Scope Summary

Mobile App (Android & iOS)

  • Attendance marking via biometric, face recognition, GPS, and QR codes
  • Notifications for check-ins, approvals, and attendance anomalies
  • Leave requests, approvals, and balance tracking
  • Access to attendance history and reports

Web-based Admin Panel

  • Employee management with role-based access controls
  • Shift scheduling, leave management, and policy settings
  • Real-time dashboards and attendance analytics
  • Reporting tools with export options for payroll and audits

Security & Compliance

  • Encrypted data storage and secure communication channels
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Audit trails and activity logs for accountability

Out of Scope (Initial Release)

  • Deep integrations with external HR or ERP systems beyond API links
  • Advanced analytics using AI (can be considered in later phases)
