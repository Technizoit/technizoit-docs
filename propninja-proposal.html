Introduction

This proposal outlines the design and development of a Lead Management System for a real estate channel partner firm that manages multiple projects and ad campaigns across Google Ads, Meta Ads, and offline sources.

The system will enable centralized management of projects, campaigns, and leads, automate lead capture from digital sources, and streamline the sales workflow from lead assignment to closure through both a Web Application (Admin / Manager) and a Mobile Application (Sales Executives).

Objectives

  • Automate lead capture from Google Ads and Meta Ads
  • Streamline assignment of leads to sales teams via smart routing rules
  • Enable efficient follow-ups through integrated call workflows
  • Track performance of campaigns and teams through analytics dashboards
  • Improve accountability with time-stamped call logs, visit proofs, and closure workflows

Scope of Work

🖥️ Web Application (Admin + Sales Manager)

1. Project & Campaign Management

  • Create and manage projects / properties
  • Link projects with Google and Meta ad campaigns
  • Define budget, location, price range, and other metadata
  • Monitor lead inflow and campaign performance per project

2. Lead Capture & Integration

  • Real-time lead ingestion via Google Ads API, Meta Lead Ads Webhooks, Website forms
  • CSV / Excel manual lead uploads
  • Automatic de-duplication and source tagging

3. Lead Assignment

  • Auto-assignment rules by project, location, or lead source
  • Manual override for re-assignment
  • Instant notifications to Sales Managers and Executives

4. Dashboard & Reports

  • Overall leads by source, campaign, and project
  • Conversion rates, CPL, and performance metrics
  • Sales Manager and Executive performance analytics
  • Export reports to Excel / PDF

5. Admin & Access Control

  • Role-based permissions (Admin / Manager / Executive)
  • Manage teams, projects, campaigns, and assignments

📱 Mobile Application (Sales Executives)

1. Lead Dashboard

  • View assigned leads with filters (project, status, date)
  • Display assigned date, expected close date, and source

2. Communication & Logging

  • Click-to-call from within the app
  • Logs start / end time and duration
  • No call recording stored or managed within the system
  • Add notes, status updates, and attachments per lead

3. Site Visit Management

  • Schedule and record site visits with client details
  • Upload geo-tagged photos from the visit
  • Sync visit data to admin dashboard

4. Lead Closure Workflow

  • Mark leads as In Progress, Junk, or Closed
  • Upload Completion Form / Agreement Copy
  • Forward client info to project owner

5. Notifications & Reminders

  • Push notifications for new leads, follow-ups, and visits
  • Daily summary alerts
