PropNinja CRM & Lead Management Platform

Custom Real Estate Channel Partner Management System

Objective

The objective of this project is to design and develop a custom CRM and Lead Management Platform for PropNinja, a real estate channel partner firm, to streamline operations across lead acquisition, sales management, site visits, and transaction closures.

The system will consist of a Web Application (for Admins and Managers) and a Mobile Application (for Sales Executives), offering an integrated ecosystem that automates lead tracking, manages teams efficiently, and provides real-time visibility into the sales funnel and performance metrics.

The application will empower PropNinja to:

  • Centralize and automate lead flow from multiple sources (Meta Ads, Google Ads, manual entry)
  • Improve transparency and accountability among sales teams
  • Simplify site visit tracking, verification, and closure documentation
  • Enhance decision-making through reporting and analytics

Core Features

1. User Roles and Access

Super Admin

  • Complete system access and configuration control
  • Add/manage properties, users, budgets, and permissions
  • Access to full analytics, reports, and transaction data

Manager

  • Access limited to their respective team members
  • Ability to assign leads, track team performance, and view lead statuses
  • View and manage team-specific reports and closure details

Sales Executive

  • Mobile app access with assigned leads, call module, and visit tracking
  • Can update lead status, mark visits, and upload closure documentation

2. Lead Management

Automatic Lead Capture

  • Integration with Meta Ads, Google Ads, and manual web form entries
  • Leads directly pushed into the centralized database

Lead Assignment

  • Automated or manual assignment of leads to salespersons based on pin code, project, or region
  • Configurable daily quota (e.g., 200–300 leads/day per salesperson)

Lead Stages and Status Tracking

  • Categorization into Hot, Warm, Cold, Unqualified, Lost
  • Disqualified leads automatically removed from salesperson's bucket and reassigned to admin

Lead Interaction History

  • Call logs, timestamps, and remarks stored for each lead

3. Call and Communication Module

  • Integrated Dial Pad within the mobile application
  • Automatic recording of call duration, timestamps, and lead engagement status
  • Option to mark outcome: Not Interested, Callback, Visited, etc.

4. Site Visit Management

  • Salesperson can schedule and mark site visits through the app
  • Geo-location tracking: Visit marking allowed only within 10m radius of mapped project
  • OTP/Feedback Code Verification:
    • After visit, client receives OTP/feedback code via WhatsApp or SMS
    • Visit marked as "Verified" only after salesperson enters the correct OTP
  • Visit tracking linked with HR attendance module (for future scalability)

5. Transaction Closure Module (TCF)

  • Transaction Closure Form accessible post-sale
  • Salesperson uploads:
    • Project and unit details
    • Base price and brokerage percentage
    • Client KYC documents, booking form, cost sheet, builder confirmation
  • Admin/Manager verification workflow for each closure

6. Reporting and Analytics

  • Comprehensive dashboards for Admins and Managers:
    • Team-wise lead conversion ratios
    • Site visit to closure performance
    • Daily/weekly call reports
  • Exportable reports (CSV/PDF) for reviews and performance audits
