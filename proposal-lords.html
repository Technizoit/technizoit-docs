Project Definition & Objectives

Project Definition

Lord's Pathology aims to build a comprehensive health app similar to Tata 1mg and Apollo, where users can seamlessly book lab tests, schedule doctor consultations, view reports, and make payments ‚Äî all through their smartphones.

Primary Objectives

  • Enable users to easily book diagnostic tests and doctor appointments For example, patients should be able to select a test, choose a slot, and confirm home sample collection in minutes.
  • Provide secure and accessible medical reports and health records Users should receive notifications for available reports and be able to view or download them instantly.
  • Offer smooth payment experiences with multiple options Integration with UPI, cards, and wallets to facilitate fast and secure payments.
  • Empower healthcare providers with operational tools A real-time dashboard for managing appointments, patient history, and reports to ensure smooth service delivery.
  • Lay the foundation for future expansion The app will be built to scale with additional features like loyalty programs, health tips, or telemedicine consultations.
