Build a robust, modular, and scalable platform that fully digitizes the student lifecycle and school operations, ensuring auditability, compliance, and a delightful user experience across web and mobile interfaces.

Success Criteria

  • End-to-end admissions live across pilot schools, improving seat fill rates and reducing manual intervention
  • Enhanced fee collection with measurable improvements in DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) within 1‚Äì2 terms
  • Achieve operational reliability: 99.9% monthly uptime and <200ms P95 latency for critical flows

Scope Summary

Parents App (Web + Mobile)

  • Multi-school applications
  • Offer tracking, fee payments, receipts, enrollment summaries
  • Timetable, attendance, performance reports
  • Messaging, PTM scheduling, service requests
  • Alumni access

Teacher App (Web + Mobile)

  • Class rosters, student profiles
  • Attendance marking (daily and period-wise)

  • Announcements, issue reporting, professional development records

School Admin Panel

  • Real-time dashboards for admissions, waitlists, and cutoffs
